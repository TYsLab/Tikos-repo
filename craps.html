📋 How to Play
Phase 1: Come-Out Roll
• Roll 7 or 11 → Pass Line wins (Natural)
• Roll 2, 3, or 12 → Pass Line loses (Craps)
• Roll 4,5,6,8,9,10 → That becomes the Point
Phase 2: Point Phase
• Roll the Point number again → Pass Line wins 🎉
• Roll a 7 → Pass Line loses (Seven Out) 💀
• Any other number → keep rolling
⭐ Smart Strategy
1. Always bet Pass Line ($10-25)
2. Once point is set, take the Odds Bet (0% house edge!)
3. Place the 6 and 8 if they're not your point
4. Avoid Any 7, Any Craps, Field — they bleed your bankroll

The Odds Bet is the only bet in any casino with NO house edge. Always take it.
📊 Odds Quick Reference
Ways to roll a 7: 6 (most common)
Ways to roll a 6 or 8: 5 each
Ways to roll a 5 or 9: 4 each
Ways to roll a 4 or 10: 3 each

That's why 7 kills you after point is set.
